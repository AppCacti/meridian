# Meridian Afternoon Launch Plan

## Goal

By the end of today, Meridian should be credible enough to send to prospects and operationally ready to start booking calls.

## 12:00-1:00 PM: Make It Public

- Buy domain.
- Set up inbox.
- Replace placeholder email.
- Add calendar link if available.
- Deploy static site.
- Test mobile and desktop.

## 1:00-2:00 PM: Legal Guardrail

- Send legal-review questions to counsel.
- Ask for review of website wording, retainer outline, fee structure, outreach wording, and what not to say.

Outreach can begin softly before the agreement is signed, but do not sign/collect money before fee language is reviewed.

## 2:00-3:30 PM: Prospect List

Build first 50 prospects:

- 25 Dubai family offices, family holdings, or principals.
- 15 Abu Dhabi family offices or ADGM-adjacent offices.
- 10 advisors, lawyers, private bankers, or family-office service providers who can refer.

Use LinkedIn, DIFC ecosystem pages, ADGM ecosystem pages, family-office event speaker lists, private wealth conference pages, founder exits, and holding groups.

## 3:30-4:30 PM: Opportunity Universe

Build 20 opportunity candidates:

- Healthcare services.
- Education.
- Logistics.
- B2B software.
- Energy services.
- Founder-owned businesses with expansion or succession signals.

Shortlist 5.

Pick 3 for the first anonymized sample.

## 4:30-5:30 PM: Sample Pack

Create the first sample pack:

- Cover.
- Mandate assumed.
- 3 anonymized opportunities.
- Snapshot.
- Fit rationale.
- Risks and unknowns.
- Scope disclaimer.

## 5:30-6:30 PM: First Outreach

Send 10-15 personalized messages.

Use the opener:

> We mapped a few anonymized opportunities that look directionally relevant to your visible mandate. Worth sending the sample pack?

Only send to high-fit prospects.

- Site live:

- Prospects researched:
- Opportunities researched:
- Sample pack drafted:
- Messages sent:
- Replies:
- Calls booked:

## Next Morning

- Book calls.
- Continue prospect research.
